# SEO Strategy

## In scope
- Public homepage at `/`
- Public about page at `/about/`
- Public security overview at `/security/`
- Public security supporting pages at `/security/pci/` and `/security/report/`
- Public discovery files (`/robots.txt`, `/sitemap.xml`, `/llms.txt`, `/.well-known/security.txt`)

## Out of scope
- Authenticated CSR and admin application routes after login (`/chat`, `/kb/**`, `/admin/**`)
- Internal API routes under `/api/**`

## Target audience
- Customer service leaders, operations managers, and security-conscious evaluators assessing Truenote.

- source-backed customer service answers
- retrieval-augmented knowledge assistant
